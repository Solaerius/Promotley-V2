Exhibition Banner — Print Preview

Promotley UF  ·  Large Format Print  ·  International Expo, Riga

Subheading Evaluation — 5 Options

1 "AI-Powered Marketing for Growing Businesses" Clear value prop, but 7 words is borderline too long for distance reading. "Growing businesses" is vague and generic — doesn't differentiate.
Clarity: 7 Premium: 5
2 "Your tool for smarter marketing" CHOSEN Personal "Your" creates instant connection. "Smarter marketing" is clear and benefit-driven. Concise, memorable, reads perfectly from distance.
Clarity: 10 Premium: 9
3 "Marketing Intelligence, Powered by AI." Professional and premium. "Intelligence" is sophisticated but slightly abstract — doesn't anchor to social media. Better for B2B corporate, slightly cold for a startup stand.
Clarity: 7 Premium: 9
4 "Turn Insights Into Marketing Results" Action-oriented but reads like a CTA, not a positioning statement. Verb-first makes it harder to read from distance. "Insights" and "Results" are overused.
Clarity: 6 Premium: 5
5 "The AI Marketing Platform for Startups" Descriptive and clear, good category positioning. "Platform" is overused in tech. "For Startups" narrows the audience unnecessarily at an international expo.
Clarity: 8 Premium: 6

Print Dimensions

  • Recommended: 300 × 100 cm
  • Aspect ratio: 3:1 (horizontal)
  • Min viable: 200 × 67 cm
  • Safe margin: 3 cm on all sides
  • Bleed: +5 mm on all edges

Export for Print

  • Open SVG in Adobe Illustrator
  • Embed fonts → Outline all text
  • Convert to CMYK color space
  • Export as PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4
  • For JPG: export at 300 DPI, sRGB
  • Large format: 72–150 DPI at actual size

Design System

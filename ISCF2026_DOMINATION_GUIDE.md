## PITCH DELIVERY COACHING

### Who Pitches
The most naturally confident speaker — not necessarily the CEO. Whoever sounds **least scripted** and most genuine. Evija Šalte (jury, headhunter) will immediately detect performance vs. authenticity.

### Pause Map
```
"Every company in this room has faced the same problem: [PAUSE 1 SEC]
What do I post on social media..."

"Our AI analyzes your real performance data [PAUSE]
and gives you personalized post ideas..."

"Unlike ChatGPT [PAUSE] — Promotley doesn't give generic advice."
```

### Energy
You pitch 4th in the second group, after a break. Judges are resetting mentally. Come in with **20% more energy than feels natural**. Smile. Stand tall. Own the silence before you start.

### Eye Contact
On "Every company in this room" — make slow, genuine eye contact with 2–3 jury members. Hold it for one breath. Don't look away first.

### Timing
Practice with a timer. 127 words at a calm conversational pace = 55–58 seconds. If you need to cut something under pressure, cut the market stat line last.

### The Secret
The best pitches don't feel like pitches. Speak like you're telling a friend about a problem you solved. The product is real. The problem is real. Let that come through.

## STAND INTERVIEW Q&A

Judges will visit your stand and probe beyond the pitch. Memorize these.

---

**Q: How many customers do you have?**
> "We're in early adoption — several student companies from the UF network are onboarding right now. We're also using ISCF as a live validation event. The interest we've seen here today confirms the demand is real."

**Q: What makes you different from ChatGPT?**
> "ChatGPT gives you advice based on whatever you type in. Promotley connects directly to your social media accounts, reads your actual engagement data, and generates strategies based on what's already working for your specific business, industry, and budget. It's the difference between a generic doctor and a specialist who has all your test results."

---

**Q: How do you make money?**
> "Free tier to remove the barrier — any student company can start immediately. Premium subscription unlocks deeper analytics, unlimited content generation, and multi-platform management. As users grow their businesses, they grow with us."

---

**Q: How will you scale beyond student companies?**
> "Student companies are our proving ground — a built-in network of thousands across Sweden and Europe through JA. From there, the same product serves any small business: local cafés, boutiques, early-stage startups. The UF network is our launchpad, not our ceiling."

---

**Q: Why should Promotley win Best Company?**
> "Because we identified a real problem, built a real solution, and we understand our market better than anyone in this room. Every student company here is our potential user. We didn't just build a product — we built for ourselves and for people exactly like us."

---

**Q: What AI does it use? How does it work technically?**
> "Our AI connects via API to social media platforms, pulls real performance data — engagement rates, timing, content type — and runs it through a model trained specifically on small business marketing patterns. It then generates recommendations tailored to that business's specific profile, not generic templates."

---

**Q: What's your pricing exactly?**
> "We have a free tier that gives student companies full access to core features — there's no reason not to try it. Premium tiers scale with business size and needs, unlocking advanced analytics, more platforms, and higher usage limits. We're finalizing exact pricing based on user feedback from our early adopters."

---

**Q: Who is your competition?**
> "Existing tools like Buffer or Hootsuite help you *schedule* posts but don't tell you *what* to post or *why*. Generic AI like ChatGPT gives advice without knowing your data. Promotley is the only tool that combines real social media analytics with AI-generated strategy, built specifically for small businesses and student companies."
